NoiseNote - Definition


Realtime automatic note detection attempts to find and identify all the notes of a musical instrument solo, as it spontaneously plays the detected notes through the Midi Synthesizer.

However note detection scans are not perfect, and if the Note Detection Controls are poorly adjusted the note detector will pick up erroneous notes from other instruments -- these incorrect notes are called 'NoiseNotes'. By properly adjusting the Response Slider and Detail Slider controls, noisenotes can be greatly reduced.

See the Detecting Notes While Playing a Song - Tutorial for a better discussion on the proper use of the Response Slider and Detail Slider controls.
