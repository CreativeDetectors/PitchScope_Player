Midi Advance Slider



Dependent on the speed of your computer and the speed of Midi Devices, you may hear that the Recording and the MidiNotes are slightly out of synchronization.

This control can be toggled to adjust the synchronization of the MidiNotes relative to the play of the Recording. Increasing the value on this control will cause the MidiNotes to be played earlier in time than the musical recording.

Two different settings for the Midi Advance are actually saved: one for when the Midi Source buttons are set for DETECTION, and a different setting is saved when the Midi Source is set for NOTELIST. If you have a NoteList loaded, you can observe the two different settings by toggling the Midi Source radio buttons. The two different settings are NOT supposed to have the same value, but are specific to each Midi Source mode.
