Notelist - Definition


The main purpose of PitchScope products is to detect the notes within an instrumental solo, and then group those notes into a Notelist which can be played through a Midi Synthesizer .

Notelist Files can be saved, reloaded... also be edited and printed with some PitchScope products like PitchScope Navigator.

Since PitchScope Player is a shareware application, Navigator users who wish to distribute their NoteList transcription files upon the Internet can legally distribute PitchScope Player as a listening device for those NoteLists, as long as they abide by the laws for shareware distribution.
