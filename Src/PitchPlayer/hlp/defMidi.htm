Midi - Definition


Midi stands for "Musical Instrument Digital Interface", and is a set of commands for storing and transmitting information about music to computers and electronic devices. MIDI output devices interpret this information and use it to synthesize musical notes.

PitchScope products use Microsoft Windows' internal midi Software Synthesizer to generate the notes that you hear from the Note Detector or Notelist.

Some PitchScope products, like PitchScope Navigator, can also play their midi notes through an External Midi Device which is patched through the user's computer.
