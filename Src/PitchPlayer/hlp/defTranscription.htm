Transcription - Definition


When musicians listen to a musical recording and then write down its notes for sheet music, it is said that "they made a transcription of the song".

While PitchScope products can NOT write down notes in the notation style of sheet music, they can transcribe the notes into a Notelist as they spontaneously play the detected notes through Windows internal Midi Synthesizer.

As PitchScope Player transcribes the notes, it plays and displays them within the Revolver View's circular animation.


Some PitchScope products, like PitchScope Navigator can also create and display the transcribed Notelists in a kind of Piano Roll Format upon a DrivingView animation. PitchScope Navigator can also save and reload the Notelists to hard drive, and can also print the transcribed Notelists to paper using a Piano Roll Format.
